### KBD elements in HTML

```html
<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>C</kbd>
```

- <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>C</kbd> to copy the data.
- <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>V</kbd> to past the data.
